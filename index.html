Tier Performance Accountability
Tier I Accredited with Distinction; Accredited Districts with at least 75% of possible APR points; Accredited districts with no schools with less than 70% District CSIP
Tier IIA Accredited districts with less than 75% of possible APR points; accredited districts with 2 consecutive years of decline of 5% or more of possible APR points; accredited districts with schools that earn less than 70% of possible APR points; accredited districts with a significant achievement gap District oversight of CSIP (supports optional)
Tier IIB Tier II for two or more consecutive years Department oversight of CSIP
Tier III Provisionally accredited districts Comprehensive review; regional school improvement team; performance contract
Tier IV Unaccredited districts Student transfers enacted 167.131 RSMo; fiscal monitor approved; governance reviewed; contracts may be voided
Tier V Lapsed districts All operational elements of previous districts cease to exist
