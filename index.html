Tier Performance Accountability
Tier I
  • Accredited with Distinction;
  • Accredited Districts with at least 75% of possible APR points;
  • Accredited districts with no schools with less than 70%
 District CSIP
Tier IIA
  • Accredited districts with less than 75% of possible APR points;
  • Accredited districts with 2 consecutive years of decline of 5% or more of possible APR points;
  • Accredited districts with schools that earn less than 70% of possible APR points;
  • Accredited districts with a significant achievement gap
 District oversight of CSIP (supports optional)
Tier IIB Tier II for two or more consecutive years Department oversight of CSIP
Tier III Provisionally accredited districts
  • Comprehensive review;
  • Regional school improvement team;
  • Performance contract
Tier IV Unaccredited districts
  • Student transfers enacted 167.131 RSMo;
  • Fiscal monitor approved;
  • Governance reviewed;
  • Contracts may be voided
Tier V Lapsed districts All operational elements of previous districts cease to exist
